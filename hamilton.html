Our Guests from Broadway

The Hamilton Virtual Experience will feature three guests from Broadway.

Gregory Treco
Burr

Originally from the Bahamas, Gregory Treco has used his incredible talent to work his way up to Broadway stardom. Most recently, he was seen in the “Room Where It Happens,” starring as Aaron Burr & George Washington in the Broadway and Chicago companies of Hamilton. Gregory made his Broadway debut in Taboo, and originated the lead role of Zanna in the Off-Broadway production of Zanna Don’t. Gregory loves to connect to his Hamilton fans through Q&As. Ask him about his ten-year break from the business after making his Broadway debut!

Julia Harriman
Eliza

Julia is a multi-talented singer, actress, and recording artist. She has had the opportunity to play all three Schuyler sisters as a standby in the North American Tour of Hamilton, before taking over Eliza full-time in 2018. Her film and television credits include High School Musical 3, Camp Rock, and Malcolm in the Middle. Be sure to ask her what it was like to be one of Eliza's featured in our personal favorite of Lin’s Hamildrops recordings, “First Burn”!

Anthony Medina
Laurens/Philip

Anthony Lee Medina is a Hamilton MVP (3 years on Broadway!) and a well-rounded rising star. If you didn’t witness his heartbreaking performance as Laurens/Philip, you might recognize him from Spring Awakening, Netflix’s I-Land, or Starz’s Flesh & Bone. Anthony loves connecting with fans to talk about Ham and his budding career as a television writer. Be sure to ask him about working with Paul Simon!
